Events

With the goal to promote research and development in language technology in Australia and New Zealand, we organise workshops and courses.

The main goal of the ALTA workshop is to serve as a forum for Australian and New Zealander people actively participating in Language Technology activities or simply interested in them. Often the workshop includes tutorials and presentations by invited international speakers. Occassionally there are summer schools with courses at beginner and advanced levels. The main audience of the summer schools is postgraduate students and researchers in academia and industry.

To receive updated information about these and further events please subscribe to the ALTA-Announce mailing list.
