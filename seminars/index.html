ALTA Online Monthly Seminar Series

About the Series

ALTA hosts an online monthly seminar series featuring senior PhD students and postdocs from North America and Australia talking about all things NLP. Each session pairs one Australian speaker with one North American (or international) speaker, giving the audience a snapshot of recent research from both sides of the Pacific.

Motivation

Our motivation is two-fold — education and awareness:

  • Education: provide a regular venue for the Australasian NLP community to hear about cutting-edge work from senior PhD students and postdocs.
  • Awareness: increase the exposure of NLP research and surface it to people outside NLP and academia, including industry practitioners working with LLMs and language data.

Time and Format

When: First Thursday of each month, 9:00 AM Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST).

Equivalent times: roughly 2–4 PM Pacific Time / 5–7 PM Eastern Time the previous day, with some variation due to daylight saving.

Where: Online via Zoom.

Format: Two talks per session — one Australian speaker and one North American / international speaker.

Target Audience

The seminars are intended for a mix of:

  • Australian and New Zealand academics and researchers in NLP and related fields;
  • Postgraduate (MSc / PhD) students working on language technology;
  • Industry professionals interested in NLP and LLMs;
  • Anyone curious about current research directions in language technology.
